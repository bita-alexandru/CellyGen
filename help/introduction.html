Introduction

What is CellyGen

CellyGen is an application designed to explore Cellular Automata (CA).
You can play around and experiment with various Cellular Automata by tweaking the states and the rules in the corresponding panels and by drawing different patterns on the grid.
The program features a customizable Genetic Algorithm (GA) that is meant to assist in finding initial patterns that lead to interesting and complex results over the course of the simulation.

What are Cellular Automata

A cellular automaton (pl. cellular automata, CA) is a collection of "colored" cells on a grid of specified shape that evolves through a number of discrete time steps according to a set of rules based on the states of neighboring cells. The rules are then applied iteratively for as many time steps as desired.[1]
